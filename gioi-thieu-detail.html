Having lived in Taiwan and New Zealand and worked across the globe, Johnny's knowledge and styling are reflected in his design languages. Fluent in expressing his ideas, Johnny's provocative and unconventional vocabulary has made him a notable figure in defining design in Asia.
Always looking into the future to see how architecture and design should be, his adventure touches various disciplines and speaks through remarkable projects such as The Moving Kitchen for Taiwan Railway, Bloom Chair for Kobe Leather, A Little Museum Store for Taipei Fine Arts Museum and Penghu Ferry for Taiwan Maritime & Port Bureau.
